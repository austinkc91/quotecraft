Common HVAC Jobs You Can Quote

AC System Replacement

Quote full AC system replacements including condenser, air handler, line set, thermostat upgrade, and all labor. Include disposal of old equipment and permit fees in your line items.

Furnace Installation

Build proposals for gas furnace installations with equipment, gas line work, venting, thermostat, and startup. Include efficiency ratings in your scope of work to help customers understand the value.

Heat Pump Systems

Quote heat pump installations with indoor and outdoor units, refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and startup. Mention SEER2/HSPF2 ratings and potential energy savings.

HVAC Repairs & Service

Use the repair template for service calls: diagnostic fee, replacement parts, refrigerant charges, and labor. Set shorter warranty terms (90 days) for repair work.

Ductwork & Ventilation

Quote ductwork modifications, new runs, insulation, and sealing. Include materials, labor, and any necessary permits.
