Full Roof Replacement

Quote complete tear-off and replacement. Break out shingles (by square), underlayment, ice/water shield, drip edge, ridge vent, flashing, tear-off labor, install labor, dumpster, and permit.

Storm Damage Repair

Create estimates for insurance-related repairs. List affected areas, replacement materials, and labor. Include a line for matching existing shingles and any code-required upgrades.

Flat/Commercial Roofing

Quote TPO, EPDM, or modified bitumen installations. Include membrane, insulation, fasteners, flashing, and labor. Specify warranty terms for the membrane system.

Gutter & Fascia

Add gutter replacement, fascia repair, or soffit work to your roofing proposals. Quote materials and labor per linear foot.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I quote by the square?

Yes. Set the quantity to the number of squares and the unit price to your per-square rate. QuoteCraft auto-calculates the total for each line item.

Does it handle insurance work?

QuoteCraft creates clean proposals suitable for presenting to homeowners and adjusters. For Xactimate-formatted estimates, you'll need specialized insurance software — but QuoteCraft is great for your customer-facing proposal.

Can I include photos?

Not yet — QuoteCraft focuses on clean text-based proposals with line items. Photo documentation support is on the roadmap.
