Common Plumbing Jobs You Can Quote

Water Heater Installation

Quote tank or tankless water heater installations. Include the unit, supply lines, gas/electric connections, venting, expansion tank, and labor. Add permit fees where required.

Whole-House Repiping

Build detailed estimates for copper or PEX repiping. Break out materials by footage, fittings count, fixture connections, drywall access/repair, and labor hours.

Drain & Sewer Work

Quote drain cleaning, sewer line repair, or replacement. Include camera inspection, excavation, pipe materials, backfill, and restoration as separate line items.

Fixture Installation

Create estimates for bathroom or kitchen remodels. List each fixture (faucet, toilet, sink, tub, shower valve) with materials and per-fixture labor costs.

Gas Line Work

Quote gas line installations for ranges, dryers, fireplaces, or generators. Include pipe, fittings, pressure testing, and inspection fees.
