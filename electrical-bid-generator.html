Common Electrical Jobs You Can Quote

Panel Upgrades

Quote 100A to 200A panel upgrades or sub-panel installations. Include the panel, breakers, wire, conduit, grounding, permit, and inspection fees as separate line items.

EV Charger Installation

Create estimates for Level 2 EV charger installations. Include the charger (if supplying), dedicated circuit, wire run, breaker, outlet/hardwire, and any required panel upgrades.

Whole-House Rewiring

Build detailed bids for rewiring older homes. Break out by room or circuit: wire, devices (outlets, switches, fixtures), boxes, and labor. Include drywall repair and inspection.

Commercial Lighting

Quote commercial lighting installations or retrofits. List fixtures, controls, wiring, mounting hardware, and labor separately. Include energy savings estimates in your scope.

Generator Installation

Create estimates for standby generator installations. Include the transfer switch, fuel connections, electrical connections, concrete pad, permit, and startup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this for commercial bids?

Yes. While QuoteCraft is designed for simplicity, you can add as many line items as you need. For complex commercial bids, you may want to group items by system or phase.

Does it include material markups?

You control the pricing. Enter your marked-up material costs as the unit price. Many electricians add a 15-25% markup on materials — just factor that into your line item prices.

Can I save templates for repeat jobs?

Company defaults are saved automatically. For job templates, use the built-in trade templates as a starting point. Full template saving is coming in a future update.
